Prayer for the Long Life of Kyabgön Sakya Trichen

by Khenchen Ngawang Lodrö Rinchen


ngawang gyutrul lha rik khön gyi dung
Lord of Speech, magical emanation of the noble Khön family,
dro kün ga dzé tekchen do ngak lam
You bring happiness to all beings through the sūtra and tantra paths of the great vehicle,
pendé pal du barwé trinlé chen
Your enlightened activity brings tremendous benefit and happiness,
sampel wang gi gyalpo shyabten sol
Wish-granting king of power, may your life be secure and long!
